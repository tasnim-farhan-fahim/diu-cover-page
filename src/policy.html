Privacy Policy for DIU Cover Page


Welcome to DIU Cover Page (diucoverpage.com), a student‑built academic cover page generator for learners of Daffodil International University (DIU). Your privacy and the security of your data are important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information, our approach to data protection, and how advertising is served on this site.

1. Purpose of the Site

This website is intended solely to help DIU students create academic cover pages for assignments, lab reports, and other submissions. It is operated by an individual owner and is not part of, sponsored by, or affiliated with any commercial organization.

2. Information Collection and Use

  • No personal data collection: DIU Cover Page does not intentionally collect, store, or process personal data. The information you enter into forms is used only to generate your cover page and is not transmitted to our servers or shared with any third party.
  • Academic use only: All information you provide is used solely for creating academic cover pages and remains within your browser. We do not require registration, login, or any form of personal identification.

3. Data Security

  • Because we do not collect or store user data on our servers, the risk of a data breach or unauthorized access from our side is minimized.
  • Cover page generation is performed locally in your browser. We do not transmit your input data to any backend system.

4. Cookies and Tracking

  • DIU Cover Page does not use cookies, analytics tools, or similar technologies to track or profile individual users for its own purposes.
  • Your use of the cover page generator is private within your browser. Any cookies or tracking technologies used by third‑party services (such as advertising providers) are governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Advertising Policy

  • This site may display advertisements provided by Google or other reputable advertising networks.
  • We do not knowingly permit ads that contain sensitive, inappropriate, or offensive content. If you see an ad you believe is unsuitable, please notify the site administrator.
  • Advertising revenue helps support the ongoing maintenance and improvement of this free academic tool.
  • We do not share user-entered data with advertisers. Any ad targeting is managed directly by the ad provider (for example, Google) and does not use data collected from this tool.

6. Third‑Party Links

  • Our site may contain links to external websites for additional resources or reference. We are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices of these third‑party sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

7. Children’s Privacy

  • DIU Cover Page is designed for university‑level students and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. User Rights and Control

  • You retain full control over any information you type into the site. We do not store it beyond your current browser session.
  • If you have questions or concerns about how your information is handled, you are encouraged to contact the site administrator.

9. Changes to This Policy

  • We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page and will take effect from the date indicated below.

10. Contact Information

  • For questions, concerns, or to report an issue (including inappropriate advertisements), please contact the sole owner and administrator using the links provided on this website.

11. Additional Information

  • DIU Cover Page aims to provide a safe, focused, and academic‑oriented environment for DIU students, with minimal and relevant advertising where necessary.
  • We do not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose user‑provided information to third parties.
  • By using this site, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms and Conditions.
