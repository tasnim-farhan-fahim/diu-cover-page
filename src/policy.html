Privacy Policy for DIU Cover Page

Welcome to DIU Cover Page(diucoverpage.com), student-created academic cover page creation tool for students of Daffodil International University (DIU). Your privacy and security are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how we handle your information, our commitment to data protection, and our advertising practices.

1. Purpose of the Site

This website is designed exclusively for DIU students to create academic cover pages for assignments, lab reports, and submissions. The site is operated by a sole owner and is not affiliated with any commercial entity.

2. Information Collection and Use

  • No Personal Data Collection: DIU Cover Page does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. All form data entered is used only for generating cover pages and is not transmitted, saved, or shared with any third party.

  • Academic Use Only: All information entered is for academic purposes and remains on your device. We do not require registration, login, or any form of identification.

3. Data Security

  • Since no user data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data breach or unauthorized access from our side.
  • All cover page generation is performed locally in your browser. We do not transmit your data to any server.

4. Cookies and Tracking

  • DIU Cover Page does not use cookies, web beacons, or any tracking technologies to monitor user activity.
  • Your browsing experience is private and anonymous.

5. Advertising Policy

  • This site may display advertisements provided by Google or other reputable ad networks.
  • We do not allow ads containing sensitive, inappropriate, or offensive content. If you encounter any such ads, please contact the site administrator immediately.
  • Ads help support the maintenance and development of this free academic tool.
  • No user data is shared with advertisers. Ad targeting is handled by Google and does not involve any data from this site.

6. Third-Party Links

  • Our site may contain links to third-party websites for reference or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

7. Children's Privacy

  • DIU Cover Page is intended for university students and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. User Rights and Control

  • You have full control over the information you enter. Nothing is stored or processed beyond your browser session.
  • If you have concerns about privacy or data usage, please contact the site administrator.

9. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with the effective date.

10. Contact Information

  • If you have questions, concerns, or wish to report inappropriate ads, please contact the sole owner and administrator at links below.

11. Additional Information

  • DIU Cover Page is committed to providing a safe, ad-free (except for Google ads), and academic-focused environment for DIU students.
  • We do not sell, rent, or disclose any information to third parties.
  • By using this site, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms and Conditions.
