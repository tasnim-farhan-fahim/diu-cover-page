📘 How to Create Your DIU Cover Page

  1. Choose Your Cover Page Type:
    Select the appropriate form for your needs from the options above (📄 Assignment, 📑 Lab Report, 📝 Final Lab Report, or 📂 Lab Report Index).
  2. Fill Out the Form:
    ✍️ Enter all required information such as your name, ID, course details, teacher’s name, and any other relevant fields. Double-check for accuracy!
  3. Generate Your Cover Page:
    ⚡ After filling the form, click the “Generate” button. Your cover page will be created instantly.
  4. Download or Print:
    ⬇️ You can download your cover page as a PDF document or an Image for submission.
  5. View Last Submission:
    👀 If you need to review your last submission, use the “View Last Submission Result” link above.
  6. Need Help?
    💡 For any issues or feedback, use the contact links in the bottom to reach out.
