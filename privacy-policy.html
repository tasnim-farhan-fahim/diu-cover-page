Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Welcome to DIU Cover Page , a student‑built academic cover page generator for learners of Daffodil International University (DIU). Your privacy and the security of your data are important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information, our approach to data protection, and how advertising is served on this site.

1. Purpose of the Site

This website is intended solely to help DIU students create academic cover pages for assignments, lab reports, and other submissions. It is operated by an individual owner and is not part of, sponsored by, or affiliated with any commercial organization.

2. Information Collection and Use

  • No personal data collection: DIU Cover Page does not intentionally collect, store, or process personal data. The information you enter into forms is used only to generate your cover page and is not transmitted to our servers or shared with any third party.
  • Academic use only: All information you provide is used solely for creating academic cover pages and remains within your browser. We do not require registration, login, or any form of personal identification.

3. Data Security

  • Because we do not collect or store user data on our servers, the risk of a data breach or unauthorized access from our side is minimized.
  • Cover page generation is performed locally in your browser. We do not transmit your input data to any backend system.

4. Cookies and Tracking

  • DIU Cover Page does not use cookies, analytics tools, or similar technologies to track or profile individual users for its own purposes.
  • Your use of the cover page generator is private within your browser. However, third-party services (such as advertising providers) may use cookies as detailed below.

5. Google AdSense & Advertising Policy

To support the ongoing maintenance and improvement of this free academic tool, this site displays advertisements provided by Google AdSense and its partners. As part of this service, please be aware of the following:

  • Third-party vendors , including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Opting Out: Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings
  • We do not collect user-entered form data (like Student IDs or Names) so there are no chance of sharing it with advertisers. Ad targeting is managed directly by Google.

6. Third‑Party Links

  • Our site may contain links to external websites for additional resources. We are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices of these third‑party sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

7. Children’s Privacy

  • DIU Cover Page is designed for university‑level students and is not intended for children under 13. We do not collect information from children.

8. User Rights and Control

  • You retain full control over any information you type into the site. We do not store it beyond your current browser session.
  • If you have questions or concerns about how your information is handled, you are encouraged to contact the site administrator.

9. Changes to This Policy

  • We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page and will take effect from the date indicated below.
