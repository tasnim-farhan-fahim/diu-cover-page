Frequently Asked Questions

What paper size should I use for printing?

Note: The generated cover page is formatted for A4 size (210mm × 297mm). For correct printing or exporting, make sure your document page size is also set to A4.

Can I re-download the cover page if I close the browser?

If you accidentally close the browser or navigate away, you can still access your previous generated cover page. Head over to the Create tab and click View Last Submission Result to quickly load it.

Is it completely free?

Yes! The DIU Cover Page tool is 100% free for all students to generate and download assignments, lab reports, and lab indexes.
