May 5, 2026 Update

DIU Cover Page v2.0 is Live!

Welcome to the completely redesigned DIU Cover Page platform. We have moved to a modular, lightning-fast architecture with a beautiful glassmorphism interface. Navigating on mobile is now smoother than ever. Enjoy the seamless experience!

April 30, 2026 Announcement

Supporting Privacy and AdSense Compliance

To keep this tool 100% free for all students, we have introduced non-intrusive AdSense support along with strict privacy policies to ensure your academic data remains entirely on your device. We do not store any of your student ID, names, or course data on our servers.

December 27, 2025 Feature

New Feature: Save Your Last Submission

Tired of typing the same course and teacher details every week? You can now instantly reload your previous cover page settings with our new "Last Submission" recovery feature available in the Create Hub. Simply click the View Last Submission button to auto-fill the forms based on your previous session.

December 27, 2025 Feature

New Feature: Smart Autofill

Our new Smart Autofill feature securely saves your personal and course details directly to your device, making it faster than ever to create Assignments, Lab Reports, and Combined Reports!

Important Note: Creating an Index Page currently resets your saved autofill data. To turn autofill back on, simply create any other type of cover page, and your details will be saved again for future use.
